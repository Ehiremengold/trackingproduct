Ocean Frieght

Through our vast international network, The Freight Company's points of presence extend to all major seaports throughout the world. Working within this network and utilizing our multiple carrier partnership agreements, we are able to provide our customers with reliable and cost effective transport solutions that minimize transit times, optimize routing and scheduling, and maximize the overall effectiveness of your supply chain. Our services are complemented with readily available warehouses in case there is need to temporarily store goods. To supplement our traditional ocean freight services, we offer our customers a "sea-air" service from Asia. This service combines the economic advantage of ocean freight with the speed of air freight.

Our solutions aim to grant you the benefit of flexible market entry and exit, key to meeting the challenges of today's competitive global environment. Our team works closely with our customers to understand their business, its needs, goals and strategies and is committed to delivering exceptional, personalized and flexible services for all transportation and logistics needs.
